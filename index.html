RESEM

Indulge in the intellectual fervor of the Research-Based Hackathon—an exhilarating and intense affair meticulously crafted for researchers, innovators, and the avant-garde of creative thought. Over the course of a riveting 26 hours, participants will immerse themselves in the exploration of their research passions, whether they be grounded in reality or sprung from the realms of imagination(WHAT IF). The challenge lies in crafting a condensed opus—a mini research paper—that encapsulates their profound findings.

Within this crucible of intellect, teams shall engage in a spirited competition, vying for supremacy as they unveil their research concepts, avant-garde ideas, and the seamless execution of their projects. This event transcends the ordinary, providing an extraordinary platform to not only showcase your research prowess but also to forge collaborative alliances with similar minded people, transforming nascent ideas into tangible reality. Catch the wave of innovation, ride the currents of creativity, and let your intellect soar in the Research-Based Hackathon—a celestial convergence of minds.

HACKAUCTION

Prepare for the ultimate clash of coding prowess at HackAuction! The competition unfolds in two dynamic groups: Group A delves into the world of front-end development, weaving magic with Java, CSS, and HTML to craft jaw-dropping websites. Following this creative frenzy, an epic frameworking battle ensues as Group A and Group B go head-to-head in a virtual arena, pushing their limits and giving their all. The victorious teams from each group advance to the next round, where they present their stellar work to a panel of industry experts. This golden opportunity allows participants to showcase their skills, impress the judges, and earn well-deserved recognition.

The judging process is intense, with experts meticulously analyzing creativity, functionality, and overall execution. As the tension rises, the panel decides who deserves the crown of victory. Buckle up for an adrenaline-pumping journey at HackAuction : a celebration of talent, competition, and boundary-pushing in the coding universe!
